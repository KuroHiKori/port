Who is Kai?

Kai is a young digital artest.

As you can probably tell by how, I am Kai! I am interested in 3D, animation, coding, etc. If it uses computers I want to learn about it and use it to make new and cool things.

3D Design

When I first came to college I wanted to come to learn how to make 3D models for videogames and films. During my time at college I learned to use software like Mudbox, 3DsMax and Blender.

Coding

My first coding class was in high school and I hated it so much when I was learning it there. At college I learned that the software we were using in high school was out of date and in only a few days of collge I learned to really like coding using programs like Brackets and Visual Studio Code.

Video Editing

Video editing was a class I remember being very exited for. Learning to put together videos to make content for others to watch and how to edit clips to look right was really cool. Before coming to college I had no idea how to edit things like that but now I know how to use After Effects, edit sounds, foley, color correction and lots more.

Photography

Photography was never something I wanted to do before coming to collge and it still isn't but I can't denie that learning it was a very important skill going forwards. There are a lot of things that I didn't know photography played into. I know now.

What have I done?

In college I have had the privilege of being in all kinds of classes.

I have been in everything from photography to web design to 3D. I have even done things like video editing, app creation and video game design / coding.

Videos

With the skills I learned during my time at college I was able to create much more profesional looking videos using all kinds of effects both 2D and 3D alike. To see some of these please go watch some of my stuff on YouTube.

Photoshop

Everyone can do photoshop in todays world. It really isn't that impressive. The difference between someone who just dose it for fun and someone who learned the ins and outs in college however is that I now know plenty of tricks and technics to insure the photos I am touching up look true to life.

Web Design

To see what I learned in terms of web design look no further then the page you are on right now. To make this I used a free template online and from there I made it my own, adding my own words, picutes, links and even my favicon. =;'~';=

3D Art

If you keep scrolling down you will see a photo of a robotic dinosaur I was able to make in 3DsMax. The prosess didn't take as long as one may think because of what I learned in class. The model is fully rigged and even has it's own roaring animation at the time of writing this.

UI/UX Design

UI is creating a nice clean and easy to use experience for people. Like this site for example, it's easy to use and has simple navigation. I am extra careful to make sure everything I make has a clean lay out and navigation for people using it.

Mobile Development

Creating mobile apps is a huge skill in todays world. Everyone today has a phone so the ability to make things that can be used on a phone is very valuable. This is something I learned in college and would posably like to work on in the future as well if the road takes me there.
